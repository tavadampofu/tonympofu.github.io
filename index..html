Projects

A collection of garments and accessories created entirely from reclaimed fabrics. This project focused on transforming discarded textiles into high-quality, contemporary fashion pieces while promoting sustainable consumption.

Community Textile Workshop Series

2022-2023

Developed and led workshops teaching textile upcycling techniques to local communities. Participants learned practical skills for extending the life of their clothing and creating new pieces from existing materials.
